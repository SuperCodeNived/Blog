Places You Must Visit

Universal Studios Hogwarts Castle

I visited on June 17th, 2022

"The Wizarding World of Harry Potter is a themed area spanning two theme parks—Islands of Adventure and Universal Studios Florida—at the Universal Orlando Resort in Orlando, Florida. The area is themed to the Harry Potter media franchise, adapting elements from the film series and novels by J. K. Rowling. The Wizarding World of Harry Potter was designed by Universal Creative from an exclusive license with Warner Bros. Entertainment. I had a really good time at the park, becayse the ride was very interesting, the visual effects looked convincing, and castle was built with meticulous detail. I especially enjoyed the use of cgi to display parts of the story, alongside numerous practical sets to bring a unique feel to the park.

Gooseberry Falls

I visited on August 8th, 2021

Gooseberry Falls is a natural wonder. It consists of 3 levels of waterfall, which leads to crashing water against rocks. You can swim near the fall, and witness all three layers. It is a sight to behold and a beautiful place. 100% recommend, especially in the evening.

Statue of Liberty

I visited on April 3rd, 2022

The Statue of Liberty (Liberty Enlightening the World; French: La Liberté éclairant le monde) is a colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor in New York City, in the United States. The copper statue, a gift from the people of France to the people of the United States, was designed by French sculptor Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi and its metal framework was built by Gustave Eiffel. The statue was dedicated on October 28, 1886. I had a great time, though it was a bit smaller than I expected. It is a wonderful peice of arcitecture, and has a high value on craftsmanship, and detail. My Rating: 4/5

Movie Reviews

Interstellar
Outstanding film from on the best directors working today

watched the film 2 years ago, and was blown away by the quality. It has one the most powerful stories I've ever seen, and was made with real heart. It also has a perfect use of pracical effects, and cgi. This leads to no wonky effects anywhere. The score is also magnificent(obvisously, it's Hans Zimmer), and it adds depth to the film. All in all, I have nothing negitive to say. 5/5

Gladiator
Smart, Intense, and Intelligent film

Gladiator is a film i've seen many times. Riddley Scott and his team have found a way to make something special. It has and all star cast, and smart story, and some of the best cinematography I've ever seen. And keep in mind this film was make in 2000, and it started a whole phase of ancient rome movies. The villian Commodous is so creepy, that you get genuine fear for the characters around him. I can't say much more, but this movie deserves the 6 oscars that it won.

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King
The perfect end to a trilogy

This movie is outstanding. It swooped the 2004 oscars, winning 11, the most in history. It's celebrated as the best film of the trilogy, even at it's minnimum length of 3 and a half hours. Director Peter Jackson managed to craft a film faithful, but not a slave to Jrr Tolkiens epic novel. The film has everything you can ask for. 5/5
